Debut Novel — 2026

Prophet of Rage

A Cyber Thriller

Boston needed a hero. The critical infrastructure of Massachusetts — power grids, water systems, financial networks — is under siege from a cyberattack no one saw coming. The city's fate rests on a single cybersecurity engineer who has spent his career preparing for exactly this moment.

He has the will. He has the courage. But does he have the time? Prophet of Rage is a story Martin Pina can tell — a thriller only an insider could tell, set against the streets and systems of Boston.

A Note from the Author

"I didn't write this story to entertain. I wrote it because Boston needed someone to tell the truth about what was at stake — and what it cost to stop it."

Prophet of Rage draws on Martin Pina's years working inside the cybersecurity infrastructure of one of America's most storied cities. He founded the cyber security program for the Massachusetts Department of Transportation and the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (The MBTA). The Chief Information Security Officer of one of the country's largest and oldest subway systems, the Registry of Motor Vehicles, the Highway Division, the Aeronautics Division and the Divison of Tolls for over 5 years without a single breach. It is the story he was uniquely positioned to tell — and the one he had to.
